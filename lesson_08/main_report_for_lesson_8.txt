Scaling is changing the range of numerical features.


1: StandartScaler 

from sklearn.preprocessing import StandardScaler
scaler = StandardScaler()
X_scaled = scaler.fit_transform(X)


2: MinMaxScaler (scales between 0 and 1) very sensitive to outliers

from sklearn.preprocessing import MinMaxScaler
scaler = MinMaxScaler()
X_scaled = scaler.fit_transform(X)


3: RobustScaler    (best for data with outliers)

from sklearn.preprocessing import RobustScaler
scaler = RobustScaler()
X_scaled = scaler.fit_transform(X)
